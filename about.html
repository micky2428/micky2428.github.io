About Liang

Liang's Chinese name is: 梁媛婷 (Liang Yuan Ting), and she is also known as Heidi or Hermosa.

Liang was born in Kaohsiung, Taiwan. She holds a M.S. degree in Public Affairs from National Taiwan University (NTU) and B.S. degrees in Foreign Languages and Literature from National Cheng Kung University (NCKU).

After graduation, she worked as a civil servant at the Bureau of Standards, Metrology, and Inspection (BSMI). Due to her responsibilities in handling regulatory violations and implementing government directives, she excelled in communication and collaboration with others. Additionally, she enjoys using programming to streamline work processes and increase efficiency.

Her interests include hiking, dancing, and learning new technologies.
